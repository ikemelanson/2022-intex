DASH

Stands for dietary approach to stop hypertension. Dash is designed to help treat people with high blood pressure.

Plant-Based

A Vegetarian like diet that directs you to consume your nutrient intake from plant-based resources.

Managing Nutrients

Managing Nutrients such as Phosphrous, Potassium, Protien and Sodium are big factors in preventing kidney disease.

Eating right for your Kidneys

First Step to eating right: Choose and prepare foods with less salt and Sodium
Second Step: Eat the right amount and the right types of Protein
Third Step: Choose foods that are healthy for your heart
Fourth Step: Choose foods and drinks with Phosphrous
Fifth Step: Choose foods with the right amount of potassium

The Mission

Prevent kidney and urinary tract diseases, improve the health and well-being of individuals and families affected by these diseases, and increase the availability of all organs for transplantation.
